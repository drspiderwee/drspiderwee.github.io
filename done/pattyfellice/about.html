ABOUT PATTY FILICE

In real estate, reputation is everything. Mine is built on over 30 years of experience working as a real estate broker in South County and the Bay Area. I’ve stayed with it this long because I still love what I do! I truly enjoy working closely with clients throughout the entire home buying and selling process, and I thrive on the challenge of making every transaction go as smoothly as possible.

I am fortunate to have a licensed assistant who helps me handle every detail and provide constant communication at every step along the way. It’s important to make sure that all of our clients feel special—because they are!

I’ve partnered with Intero Real Estate since the company was founded in 2002 and am proud to have closed Intero’s very first sale for the company. My clients benefit from Intero’s strength, market presence, and sophisticated marketing tools, combined with my own experience and expertise in the local area.

I represent both sellers and buyers of homes and land throughout the Bay Area. I specialize in high-end residential listings and development properties in Morgan Hill, Gilroy and San Martin, but I also serve a wide range of clients throughout South County and in Los Gatos, Campbell, and San Jose.

Having lived in Gilroy since 1979, I am deeply rooted in the communities that I serve. I am a longtime member of the Gilroy Foundation and a member of the Gilroy Assistance League and Silicon Valley Capital Club. I am currently serving as chairman on the Morgan Hill Advisory Board for Pinnacle Bank.

I am proud of my local connections and outstanding reputation—and ready to go to work for you!
