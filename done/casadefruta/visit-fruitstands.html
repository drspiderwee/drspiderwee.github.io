OUR FRUIT STANDS & STORES

Fruit Stand

Visit the Fruit Stand where Casa de Fruta began! Select fresh-off-the-farm fruits and vegetables, premium dried fruits and nuts, and unique condiments. The simplicity of an era of old-fashioned goodness and no-nonsense value is found at our original Fruit Stand.

Casa de Wine

Browse through our gourmet groceries and full-service deli section at Casa de Wine. Sandwiches and box lunches are available any time. Catered "Deli Fantasy" buffet lunches available for groups with prior arrangement. Open year round, except Christmas Day. Sample our award winning wines from Zanger Vineyards , including our classic Cabernet or our unique fruit wines (apricot, blackberry, cherry, pomegranate, raspberry) perfect with dessert or as a beginning in wine coolers. Click here for more information on Zanger Vineyards, our wine list or purchasing wine from Casa de Fruta.

Casa de Sweets

All the candy you can imagine! Taffy, chocolate covered fruit and nuts, peanut brittle, fudge, caramel apples, and much, much more! A candy lover's paradise.
