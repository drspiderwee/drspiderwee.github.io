Employment Opportunities at Casa de Fruta

Casa de Fruta is an equal opportunity employer. We conduct pre-employment criminal background checks and drug testing at the time of offer. Employment is contingent upon successfully passing both requirements. In addition, Casa de Fruta maintains a strict policy regarding exposed tattoos. Applicants who are unable to cover tattoos with an article of clothing, while in compliance with the required uniform policy, will be deemed non-hirable.
